Discover stonehaven, a charming coastal town in the north east of Scotland. From historic landmarks like Dunnotar Castle to scenic coastal walks, there's something for everyone to enjoy. See what stonehaven has to offer by clicking on any one of the cards!

Outdoor Pool

Stonehaven Open Air Pool is the UK's only art deco Olympic sized sea water lido. The water is heated and is open from late May to early September.

Dunnotar Woods

Stonehaven’s lively community wood is full of surprises and stories about the Kennedy family who once lived in Dunnottar House. Walk directly into the woodland from the town and quickly lose yourself in history and nature.

Fireballs

The Fireball Ceremony is Stonehaven’s unique way to welcome in the New Year and greet friends and neighbours. As the midnight chimes ring out on December 31st, approximately 40 men and women parade up and down the High Street swinging fiercely flaming balls around their heads.

Auntie Betties

Aunty Bettys is a lovely local business in Stonehaven which sells sweets, ice creams, hot drinks and a lot of dog-friendly treats as well!

The Bay

Award winning fish and chips shop situated on the beachfront overlooking the sea

Dunnotar Castle

Perched atop a 160 foot rock and surrounded on three sides by the North Sea, these dramatic and evocative cliff-top ruins were once an impregnable fortress of the Earls Marischal, once one of the most powerful families in Scotland.
